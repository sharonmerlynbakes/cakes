Our Story

Meet Sharon Merlyn

Welcome to my sweet world! I'm Sharon Merlyn, and baking has been my passion for over a decade. What started as a hobby in my grandmother's kitchen has blossomed into Sharon's Exotic Cakes and More.

Every cake I create tells a story - your story. From the first sketch to the final decoration, I pour my heart into crafting unique, delicious masterpieces that make your special moments even more memorable.

My specialty lies in exotic flavors that transport your taste buds on a journey around the world. Whether it's a passion fruit mousse cake or a cardamom-rose creation, I believe every celebration deserves something extraordinary.

What makes us unique: Personal consultation for every order, use of premium ingredients, and attention to dietary requirements including vegan and gluten-free options.
